How SmartCart Works

A seamless shopping experience from start to finish

1

Grab a Smart Trolley

Select any trolley from the entrance. Each cart is equipped with load cells at the base for weight verification and a smartphone mount on the handle.

2

Scan & Shop

Use your smartphone to scan product barcodes before placing them in the trolley. The app identifies each product instantly, showing details and price.

3

Weight Verification

The smart trolley's load cells automatically verify the weight of added items, comparing it to the expected weight to ensure accuracy.

4

Digital Checkout

When you're done shopping, complete your purchase through the app. Payment is processed digitally, and your receipt is sent instantly to your smartphone.
